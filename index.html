The Physician

Meet Dr. Pandey

Dr. Anuragini Pandey — "Dr. Anu" to her patients — built Integra Medical around a rare combination: rigorous, board-certified internal medicine alongside Éblouissant, her refined aesthetics practice. The name is French for "dazzling" — and it's the standard she brings to every result, with the same precision in a wellness physical as in a Vivace® session.

A faculty physician at NYU Langone Health, Dr. Pandey trained at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai and completed advanced aesthetic-medicine training in Europe. Every aesthetic treatment at Éblouissant is performed by Dr. Pandey personally — never delegated.
